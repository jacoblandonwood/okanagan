restaurant More Dog-Friendly Kelowna Foodie Hotspots (Patios - Always Verify)

Here are some additional great places to check out, always confirm their current dog policies for patios.

Breweries (often great food trucks or kitchens):

  • Kettle River Brewing Co.: (731 Baillie Ave) Chill patio, often with Provisions Kitchen.
  • Rustic Reel Brewing Company: (760 Vaughan Ave) Welcoming patio, often has treats for dogs.
  • Red Bird Brewing: (1080 Richter St) Large outdoor space, live music sometimes.
  • Unleashed Brewing Co.: (201-880 Clement Ave) **Allows dogs inside too!** Dog-themed, dog menu.

Casual & Tasty:

  • El Taquero: (1443 Ellis St) Authentic tacos, tequila, dog-friendly patio.
  • Little Hobo Soup & Sandwich Shop: (596 Leon Ave) Great for a quick, tasty lunch on their small patio.
  • Antico Pizza Napoletana: (347 Bernard Ave) Delicious pizza, patio on Bernard.

Cideries with Patios:

  • Upside Cidery: (2550 Upside Rd) Cidery and eatery, organic farm, outdoor tables.
  • Truck '59 Ciderhouse: (3670 Glencoe Rd, West Kelowna) Wood-fired pizzas, burgers, dog-friendly outdoor tables.
